This directory containts the 164 RSL index points used in the Bayesian analysis of Piecuch et al. (2018).  The relevant index points are contained in the MATLAB file late_holocene_index_points_for_bayes_2000_150_yrBP.  This file contains index points from 23 salt marshes.  157 of the 164 sea level index points are from 22 saltmarshes contained in the Holocene sea level database for the Atlantic coast of the United States of Engelhart and Horton (2012) QSR.  Their full database can be accessed here:

https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0277379111002927

in the form of excel spreadsheets.

The final 7 sea level index points are from Nassau Landing in Northeastern Florida and were published by Kemp et al. (2014) Marine Geology

https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0025322714002187

The 7 sea level index points from Kemp et al. (2014) are contained here in this directory under the file "N Florida Index Points.xlsx"
